About Muscat Metro & MMTA

Mobility Ecosystem

Oman Vision 2040

Supports sustainable mobility and citywide integration.

The Muscat Metro brand is owned and managed by the Muscat Municipality Transit Authority (MMTA). MMTA is a municipal public organisation established to plan, develop and operate Muscat’s urban rail system.

All brand assets—including visual identity, wayfinding, communication standards and passenger experience guidelines—are defined and governed by MMTA.

MMTA serves as the central authority responsible for:

  • Metro network planning and long-term system development
  • Station design, infrastructure standards and asset management
  • Train operations, service reliability and safety oversight
  • Integrated ticketing and digital mobility platforms
  • Passenger information, signage and wayfinding
  • Urban mobility data, accessibility standards and customer service
  • Coordination with other public transport modes in Muscat

Muscat Metro is part of Oman’s broader mobility ecosystem. MMTA works collaboratively with:

MTCIT

National policy direction and sector-level regulation.

Oman Investment Authority (OIA)

Infrastructure financing and long-term investment oversight.

Asyad Group

Logistics and transport development for intermodal integration.

Mwasalat

Bus and intercity networks; coordination on multimodal connectivity and integrated ticketing.

Muscat Metro operates under a municipal ownership and professional operations model, where MMTA:

  • Owns rolling stock, tracks, stations and operational assets
  • Establishes service benchmarks, brand standards and customer service policies
  • Executes daily operations through internal divisions and specialised contractors
  • Manages maintenance, staff training and safety systems
  • Oversees commercial development within stations and transit corridors

Muscat Metro supports:

  • Sustainable urban mobility
  • Reduced road congestion and emissions
  • Improved access to education, employment and services
  • Enhanced livability and economic competitiveness
  • Multimodal transportation integration throughout the capital

The brand reflects Muscat’s commitment to a forward-looking, inclusive and globally connected urban identity.
