Brand Guidelines

This page summarises the Corporate Identity rules for Muscat Metro and MMTA.

Muscat Metro

Public-facing identity used across passenger touchpoints.

MMTA

Governance, regulation, planning and official documentation.

Dual-Logo Use

Restricted to formal contexts requiring institutional recognition.

The official and most recognisable symbol of the Muscat Metro system. Use in primary applications such as signage, customer communication and digital platforms.

Proportions, structure and spatial relationships must never be altered. Bilingual wordmark may appear depending on context.

The MMTA logo may not replace or override the Muscat Metro brand in customer-facing applications.

Dual-logo usage is permitted only in:

  • Strategic planning reports
  • Technical and engineering specifications
  • Procurement, tenders and contractual documentation
  • Government-facing communication
  • Joint announcements and formal public notices

Maintain the defined minimum clear space around the logo. Do not rearrange, resize or reposition outside the guideline rules.

On non-white backgrounds, use the negative (white) version. Be careful when placing the logo over images to preserve contrast and legibility.
